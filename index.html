Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Lucas Danielsson, a professional card magician with nearly a decade of experience bringing the magic to life. Over the years, I've performed across Sweden and ventured internationally to places like Zanzibar, as well as on cruise ships between Norway and Finland. One of my proudest moments was showcasing my skills at the 2024 Swedish Championship in Magic.

I'm passionate about magic and love to share my craft with others. I believe that magic is a universal language that can bring people together and create unforgettable memories. Whether it's a private event like a party, wedding, or baptism, I bring magic to any occasion. I also offer workshops and classes for those who want to learn the art of magic themselves.
